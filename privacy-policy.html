Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Welcome to Plantivity. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

1. We Do Not Collect Any Data

Plantivity does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information. We do not:

  • Collect your name, email, or any personal information
  • Track your usage or app interactions
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Collect device information or identifiers
  • Monitor your app usage patterns
  • Share data with third parties

2. Local Data Storage

All data created in Plantivity is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Your app preferences and settings
  • Your focus session history
  • Your plant growth progress
  • Your app blocking selections

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to sync it via iCloud (see below).

3. Optional iCloud Sync

Plantivity offers optional iCloud syncing to sync your data across your devices. This is:

  • Completely optional - you can use the app without iCloud
  • User-controlled - managed through your iOS iCloud settings
  • Encrypted - all data is encrypted by Apple's iCloud service
  • Private - we cannot access your iCloud data

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is subject to Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to this data.

4. Screen Time Permissions

Plantivity uses Apple's Screen Time API to help you block distracting apps during focus sessions. When you grant Screen Time access:

  • We can only see which apps you've selected to block
  • We cannot see your app usage history or screen time data
  • We cannot access any personal information from other apps
  • This permission is used solely for app blocking functionality
  • You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings

We do not collect or store any information from Screen Time permissions.

5. Subscription and Payment Information

All subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We:

  • Do not store your payment information
  • Do not have access to your credit card details
  • Do not process payments directly
  • Only receive basic subscription status from Apple to enable premium features

All payment and subscription data is handled by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy policy. Subscription status may be synced via iCloud for cross-device access, but this is managed by Apple, not us.

6. No Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we have no data to share. We do not:

  • Sell data to third parties (we have no data to sell)
  • Share data with advertisers (we have no data to share)
  • Use third-party analytics services
  • Integrate with data brokers or marketing platforms

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security
  • If you use iCloud sync, data is encrypted by Apple
  • We do not maintain any servers or databases that could be compromised
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data for us to provide or delete. However, you have full control over:

  • Your local app data (delete by uninstalling the app)
  • Your iCloud data (manage through iOS Settings)
  • Screen Time permissions (revoke in iOS Settings)
  • Your subscription (cancel through App Store settings)

9. Children's Privacy

Plantivity is not intended for children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any data, we cannot and do not collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Summary

In short: We do not collect any data. All your information stays on your device. We respect your privacy.
